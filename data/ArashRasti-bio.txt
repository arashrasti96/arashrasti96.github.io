Arash Rasti-Meymandi received the B.A.Sc. degree (Hons.) in electrical engineering from Yazd University, Yazd, Iran, in 2019, and the M.A.Sc. degree (Hons.) in biomedical (bioelectric) engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2021. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with The Edward S. Rogers Sr. Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Toronto, Toronto, ON, Canada.
